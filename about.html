About me

The story behind zpriv

Z'priv started back in 2016 as a private Snapchat story. It was just a place to post little clips of my day for a close circle of about 50 people.

Posting life videos every single day became a habit. Over time those daily clips turned into a personal brand, and that small audience grew into something much bigger.

Now I just post publicly. Still being who I've always been, just to a new audience.
