About me

The story behind zpriv

I'm a content creator obsessed with short-form video. What started as messing around with edits turned into a craft: studying hooks, pacing, trends, and what makes people stop scrolling and watch to the end.

Off camera, I'm a pretty simple guy. I love cars, I run on good coffee, and I'm endlessly curious about marketing and why some things catch on while others don't. All three shape how I think about every video I make.

Today I make TikToks and Reels that feel native, look clean, and rack up real views. I love the puzzle of turning a plain idea into something people want to share.
